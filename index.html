Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

A bento-style overview of the world of Demon Slayer

Story Overview

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is slaughtered by demons, leaving his sister Nezuko as the sole survivor—transformed into a demon herself. Determined to find a cure and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, facing powerful demons while uncovering the secrets behind their origin.

Main Characters

Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro is the protagonist of the series, known for his compassion, determination, and keen sense of smell. He wields Water Breathing techniques and later develops the Hinokami Kagura, a powerful style linked to his family.

Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko, Tanjiro’s younger sister, is turned into a demon but retains her protective love for her brother. She fights using her demon strength and Blood Demon Art while resisting the urge to harm humans.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu is a cowardly yet talented Demon Slayer who uses Thunder Breathing. Despite his fear, he shows incredible speed and power when unconscious or pushed to his limits.

Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke is a wild and aggressive fighter who wears a boar mask and uses Beast Breathing. Raised in the mountains, he relies on instinct and raw strength in battle.

Main Antagonist

Muzan Kibutsuji

Muzan Kibutsuji is the first demon and the primary antagonist of the series. He is responsible for creating most demons, including the one that attacked Tanjiro’s family. Muzan is ruthless, manipulative, and constantly evolves to avoid death, making him the ultimate target of the Demon Slayer Corps.

The Hashira

The Hashira are the elite swordsmen of the Demon Slayer Corps, each mastering a unique Breathing Style such as Flame, Water, Wind, Sound, and Love. They stand at the front lines of the war against demons, taking on the most dangerous missions and confronting the Twelve Kizuki, Muzan’s strongest subordinates.

Anime & Manga

The Demon Slayer anime adaptation is produced by Ufotable and is praised for its stunning animation and faithful adaptation of the manga. The story spans multiple arcs, including the Final Selection, Mount Natagumo, Mugen Train, Entertainment District, and Swordsmith Village arcs, each deepening the characters and raising the stakes.

The original manga, written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge, was serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump and has become one of the best-selling manga series of all time. Its blend of emotional storytelling, intense battles, and rich world-building has earned it a global fanbase.
